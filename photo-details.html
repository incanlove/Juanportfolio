Real Estate Photography and Virtual Tours

This project started with virtual tours which later lead to photogrpahy for the agency. AP inmobiliaria wanted to stand out from the rest of the agencies by having more attenction grabbing photos. I suggested 360 tours because it was still a new concept in Peru and during the pandemic, it provided a safer and time saving alternative compared to going in person to show each house they have. I have also helped with their social media presence but giving ideas on their posts.
